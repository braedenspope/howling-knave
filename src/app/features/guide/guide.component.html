How Training Works

The crew of the Howling Knave will teach you what they know — but only if you put in the hours. Here is how the work pays off.

schedule

Your day is eight hours

Each day on the board is eight one-hour blocks (sleep is assumed). Some hours get claimed by ship duties — the work that keeps you afloat. Whatever is left is yours to spend training with the crew.

Keep at it — the third try always lands

A run of bad luck on Short sessions won't trap you. Fail a Short session of the same training twice, and the third Short session finishes the whole training automatically — whatever the dice say.
